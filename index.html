About Rei's Cafe

Rei's Cafe is a renowned coffe shop that was established not too long ago in Bulacan, as of now it only has three branches across Bulacan. We have kind and considerate staffs that will accomodate you as you stay, and also a calming ambiance to better match your prefered orders.

Our menu

Our menu was made through hardwork and experience of trial and error, we made sure to serve only the finest of flavors that suits one's pallete. We also include the ideas of the staffs to suggest any additional that we can call "limited" products to be served, which will be permanent if rated highly by the customers

Our staffs

Our staffs are not limited to those who only have a degree, we have also hired multiple employee that are willing to learn, who has experience, and of course with a positive attitude. We do not limit ourselves to hire who has talent, but also a chance to those who we see that are passionate.
